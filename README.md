# Pilatable

Guided Pilates mat workouts on your Pebble Time 2, paced by your breath.

Pilatable turns your watch into a calm, glanceable Pilates coach. It leads your
breathing with gentle vibrations, shows a breathing ring and your progress, and
counts you through every rep and rest, so you can keep your focus on the mat
instead of on a screen.

## How it works

Build a session on your phone, then run it hands-free on your watch. The watch
remembers your last session, so you can work out even when your phone is in the
other room.

## On the watch

- **Review first, then start.** When you open Pilatable it shows the session:
  the moves, how many, and roughly how long. Press the middle button when you are
  ready to begin.
- **Breathe with the ring.** A ring expands as you breathe in and contracts as
  you breathe out, with a large count in the center and your cycle progress below.
- **Feel your breath.** Vibrations lead every breath so you do not have to look:
  one buzz to breathe in, two buzzes to breathe out. Each exercise follows its
  own breathing pattern.
- **Natural pacing.** A short reset between reps gives you room to settle, with a
  little extra time when two breaths would otherwise run together.
- **Rest, handled.** Between exercises a countdown shows how long to rest and
  which move is next, then the workout moves on by itself.
- **Simple buttons.** Up for the previous move, the middle button to pause or
  resume, Down to skip, Back to exit. No need to tap the screen.

## On your phone

Open Pilatable's settings in the Pebble app to plan your workout:

- **Browse the full repertoire.** 46 classic mat exercises grouped into six
  chapters, with search and filters for Fundamental, Intermediate, and Advanced.
- **Tune each move.** Set the number of reps, the pace of each breath, and the
  rest afterward.
- **Set your cues.** Turn vibration on or off, choose the intensity, and switch
  on a 3, 2, 1 lead-in before each set.
- **Build and send.** Add moves to your session and it syncs to your watch,
  ready to go.

## The repertoire

Six chapters take you from the basics to advanced control: Foundation,
Abdominal Work, Fine Articulation, Bridging, Side Exercises, and Back Extensions,
spanning Fundamental, Intermediate, and Advanced levels. Each exercise carries
its own breathing sequence and a few plain-language technique cues. Two classics
get special treatment: the Hundred counts its five-beat pumps, and Swimming flows
as continuous breathing.

## Getting started

1. Install Pilatable on your Pebble Time 2.
2. Open it on the watch and press the middle button to run the built-in
   Fundamental Mat session, or open its settings in the Pebble app first to build
   your own.
3. Review the plan on your wrist, press the middle button, and breathe.

## Good to know

- Made for the Pebble Time 2 (200 by 228, color), driven by the physical buttons.
- Works offline once a session is on the watch. Your phone is only needed to
  change the workout.
- Breathing and technique guidance is original content written for this app.
